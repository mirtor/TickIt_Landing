FAQ

Quick answers to common questions.

How do I install it?

TickIt is a PWA. You can install it as a shortcut:

  • Desktop: Chrome/Edge usually show an install icon in the address bar.
  • Android (Chrome): open the app → menu ⋮ → Install app or Add to Home screen.
  • iOS (Safari): tap Share → Add to Home Screen.

You can also просто bookmark it in your browser.

Are my data private?

Your tasks and notes are stored under your Firebase account and should only be accessible to you through security rules.

We’re working on end-to-end encryption so that even at the infrastructure level, your content can be even more private.

How do I get started fast?

Open the app, create your first task, and add subtasks or a bullet note.
