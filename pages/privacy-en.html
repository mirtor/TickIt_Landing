Privacy Policy

This policy explains how TickIt handles user data. If you have questions, also review your account and browser settings.

Quick summary: TickIt uses Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore to store your tasks and notes linked to your user account. We do not sell data. Administrative access to the database by the project owner is not intended to analyze personal content.

1. What data we collect

  • Authentication data: email and/or identifiers from your login provider.
  • App data: tasks, subtasks, and notes you create.
  • Basic technical metadata required for the service to function (e.g., timestamps).

2. Purpose of processing

Data is used exclusively to:

  • Enable sign-in and maintain your account.
  • Sync your tasks and notes across devices.
  • Improve app stability and user experience.

3. Technology stack

TickIt is built with Vue and uses Google Firebase services:

  • Firebase Authentication for user management.
  • Cloud Firestore to store tasks and notes.
  • App Check (reCAPTCHA Enterprise) to protect backend usage against abuse.

4. Where data is stored

Data is stored on Firebase/Google Cloud infrastructure under the project associated with TickIt. The exact location may depend on project configuration.

5. Data sharing

TickIt does not share your data with third parties for commercial purposes. Data is processed by Firebase as the infrastructure provider.

6. Security

User-based access rules are applied so each person can only view their own documents.

Important note: This app does not implement end-to-end encryption for content. Technically, the project owner could have administrative access to the database. In small personal productivity apps, this approach is common. If E2E encryption is added in the future, this policy will be updated.

7. Retention and deletion

  • You can delete tasks and notes from within the app.
  • If you want to delete your account and associated data, you can request it from the project contact.

8. Cookies and similar technologies

Firebase and reCAPTCHA may use cookies or browser storage needed for authentication and security. Behavior may vary depending on your browser and its privacy policies.

9. User rights

If you are located in Spain or the European Economic Area, you may exercise your rights of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, and portability where applicable by contacting the project owner.

10. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated to reflect security improvements, infrastructure changes, or new legal requirements.

11. Contact

If you need support or want to request account deletion, contact the owner of the repository/project where TickIt is published.
