Privacy

This is a lightweight summary for a personal productivity app.

What Tickit stores

Tickit stores task and note data associated with your account to enable syncing across devices. This may include task titles, completion state, subtasks, optional dates, links, and note descriptions.

Authentication

Authentication is handled via Firebase Authentication. Your email and user identifier are used to provide access to your data.

Data access

Data is scoped by userId in Firestore. The project owner can technically access database content unless end-to-end encryption is implemented.

Third-party services

Tickit uses Firebase services (Authentication, Firestore, Hosting) and may use App Check to help protect against abuse.

Contact

If you are using Tickit in a shared or public context and need a more formal policy, add a contact email here.
